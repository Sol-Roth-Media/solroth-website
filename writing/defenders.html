From Bar Pitch to Book: The Story Behind the Story

Sometimes, stories have stories of their own. The novel you're reading, Defenders of the Last City, began life not on a quiet desk, but on a barstool in Santa Monica.

A few years back, I was just bullshitting with the guy next to me, talking science fiction, rambling about this crazy story idea I had – a desperate family fleeing marauders across a desolate wasteland towards a gleaming, perfect domed city called Aspyria...

Suddenly, the guy – a short man in a suit – stopped me. "Hold on," he said... A few minutes later, he returned, trailing a whole group of nicely dressed, important-looking people. "Do that again," he instructed... "This is one of the best movie pitches I've ever heard."

I grabbed the stool, hopped up, and gave the pitch of my life... I dove deeper into the world – the Defender candidate program, the Network AI, the protagonist Paragon, the cybernetic upgrades making candidates less human, and the final, brutal test of morality...

The reaction was electric. Business cards appeared, cell numbers were exchanged... For a while, it felt like one of those movie moments. Hollywood can be like that – a tantalizing near-miss.

But the story wouldn't let go... So, I decided to bring Defenders of the Last City to life in a different form. What you hold now is that story, fleshed out from the screenplay that almost was.

I hope you enjoy Paragon's journey as much as I enjoyed finally telling it.

- Sol Roth

What Awaits You Inside Aspyria?

  • A Stunning Dystopian World: Explore the stark contrast between the desolate wasteland and the technologically advanced, rigidly controlled domed city of Aspyria.
  • High-Tech Augmentation: Dive deep into a world of cybernetic eyes, synthetic skin, nanobot-infused blood, cognitive implants, and the profound changes they bring.
  • Relentless Action & Brutal Training: Experience visceral combat, high-speed chases in advanced vehicles (Hornets), and deadly training simulations like the 'Marked for Death' and the 'Vortex of Existence'.
  • A Compelling Protagonist: Follow Paragon's journey from vulnerable Outlander to augmented warrior, grappling with loss, ambition, and the moral complexities of his new world.
  • Thought-Provoking Themes: Question the price of safety, the definition of humanity in an augmented age, and the dangers of unchecked power and obedience.

About the Author

Solomon Rothman knows about chasing ambitious dreams and navigating unexpected obstacles, themes that resonate deeply within *Defenders of the Last City*. The novel itself has its own origin story, born from a screenplay pitch in a Santa Monica bar that electrified studio executives before taking a different path, much like the journey of his protagonist, Paragon.

An actor, artist, and writer, Rothman brings a multi-faceted creative perspective to his storytelling. His background also includes significant experience in the tech world as a programmer and web developer, running his own development company with clients ranging from startups to major corporations like NBC Universal and the City of Los Angeles. This blend of artistic vision and technical understanding informs his exploration of Aspyria's augmented society and the complex relationship between humanity and the machines designed to save it.

Author of the non-fiction book *The Human Centered AI Imperative*, Rothman explores the intersection of technology, ambition, and the human condition with both passion and a healthy dose of hard-won realism. He understands the allure of grand visions and the often-brutal realities faced when pursuing them, whether in Hollywood, Silicon Valley, or the fictional wastelands surrounding Aspyria.

Solomon lives in Los Angeles with his wife and two children.
